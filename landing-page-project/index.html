ABOUT US

Rescue. Rehome. Restore.
Stray No More is a movement and digital platform working to rescue stray dogs and cats in the Philippines. With over 13 million strays nationwide, many animals go hungry, unloved, and unseen. We believe every single one deserves better.

Our goal is a country where no animal is left behind, and every dog and cat is protected, seen, and loved.

Let's make “stray” no more.
We partner with local shelters and animal welfare groups to

Rescue and rehabilitate strays

Provide medical care

Find loving, permanent homes

Beyond rescue, we also

Promote adoption

Educate the public

Fight the stigma against native breeds like aspins and puspins

How We Make Change

Through stories, donation drives, adoption events, and volunteer efforts, we're building a kinder, more compassionate future—especially for the next generation.
